About Sunset Harmony

Sunset Harmony, driven by the passion and vision of DJ BAI, blends the sounds of organic, afro, and melodic house to create a captivating musical journey. Founded on the sun-kissed shores of Ibiza, where electronic music finds its purest expression, Sunset Harmony has evolved into a global project encompassing events, artist collaborations, and an official label for emerging talent.

Our mission is simple: to create meaningful connections through music that resonates beyond the dancefloor. From soulful organic house to vibrant melodic and afro house rhythms, we curate sounds that capture the essence of sunsets, travels, and global inspirations.

What began as intimate gatherings on a boat in Ibiza has transformed into a platform that supports talented artists and unique events around the world. Today, Sunset Harmony bridges the Mediterranean’s rich musical heritage with the innovative spirit of Dubai, offering unforgettable experiences through carefully curated events and releases.

Whether you’re here to discover music, join our events, or become part of our creative community, Sunset Harmony invites you to immerse yourself in a world where music and magic meet.
